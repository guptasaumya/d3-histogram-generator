How to fill the form:
Select the sample size for the data.
Choose between with or without replacement for the type of sample selection.
Choose the number of bins for the histogram plot.
Choose the type of distribution.
Choose the parameters needed to generate the chosen distribution.
Click on 'Submit'.

Note:
If you do not select anything and directly click on the 'Submit' button, you look at the default distribution:- random distribution; sample size: 20; range: 1-100; bins: 20; without replacement.
Reload the page before putting in a different set of parameters.
Do not put numbers like '.1' instead put '0.1'.
